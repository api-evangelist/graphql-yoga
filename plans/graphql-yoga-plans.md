# GraphQL Yoga Plans

## Open Source (Free)

GraphQL Yoga is released under the [MIT License](https://github.com/dotansimha/graphql-yoga/blob/main/LICENSE) and is free to use in any project — personal, commercial, or enterprise — with no licensing fees.

- **Cost:** $0
- **License:** MIT
- **Source:** https://github.com/dotansimha/graphql-yoga
- **npm package:** `graphql-yoga`

## Self-Hosted Deployment

Because GraphQL Yoga is a library rather than a managed service, all hosting costs are determined by your chosen infrastructure provider (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, Cloudflare Workers, Fly.io, Vercel, Railway). There is no subscription or per-request fee from The Guild or the GraphQL Yoga project itself.

## The Guild — Paid Support & Consulting

The Guild (the organization that maintains GraphQL Yoga) offers commercial support, consulting, and implementation services for teams that want expert help.

- **Website:** https://the-guild.dev
- **Contact:** https://the-guild.dev/contact
- **Services include:** architecture review, custom plugin development, migration assistance, and ongoing support contracts

Pricing for support and consulting is negotiated directly with The Guild and is not publicly listed.
